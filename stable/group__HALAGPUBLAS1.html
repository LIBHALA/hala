Detailed Description

Wrappers to GPU-BLAS level 1 methods for vector operations. The operations are identical to the BLAS templates described in BLAS level 1 templates, the difference is that the accepted vectors sit on the CUDA device and the first parameter of the overloads is a hala::gpu_engine with active GPU-BLAS handle.

Note: the device ID of the vectors and the engine should match.
