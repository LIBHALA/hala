Detailed Description

CUDA and ROCM
The two frameworks provided by cuBlas and rocblas provide functionality that is very similar to each other and almost identical to BLAS. The difference with BLAS comes from the need to keep an additional handle (pointer to an opaque data-structure) and that the data of all vector like classes resided in device memory. HALA templates allow for the selection of one of the two and the templates can be instantiated with the correct backend. In addition, HALA provides the hala::gpu_wrapped_array, hala::gpu_vector, and hala::gpu_engine classes for manipulating memory and the opaque handles.
